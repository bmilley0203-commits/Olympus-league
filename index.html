About Olympus

Olympus R6 League is a competitive Tier 3 PC league focused on developing upcoming talent in Rainbow Six Siege. Our goal is to create a structured, competitive environment where teams can improve, compete, and gain exposure.

×

League Rules

  • No cheating or exploits (instant ban)
  • All matches must be played on time
  • Respect all players and staff
  • Roster changes must be approved
×

League Format

- Round Robin Regular Season - Top teams advance to playoffs - BO3 matches - Standard competitive R6 ruleset
